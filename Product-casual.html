Product-casual + Resort wear

  • Vishaka Jayasundara
  • Religious place resort
  • Material-super voil

This dress has been designed based on the problem of the contemporary society. That is, a dress that can be worn inside the religious place for tourists and a dress that can be worn outside. It is a simple and versatile dress suitable for women between 20-30 years old. This creation made using pre historical cave paintings adds meaning, that is, there is complexity in simplicity. The exterior is simple, but the interior is complex. So are the prehistoric paintings. The exterior looks very simple, but it is a complex reading to the inner mind. So is the Dharma. That's why prehistoric art was used to design such the clothes.
